The CNIL is a French independent administrative authority whose mission is to protect privacy and personal data. It is responsible for ensuring compliance with legislation on personal data, informing and raising awareness among citizens about their rights and responsibilities regarding personal data, and imposing sanctions on companies and organizations that do not comply with the law.

According to the CNIL, a secure password must contain at least twelve characters including at least:

  • One number
  • One uppercase letter
  • One punctuation mark or special character (dollar sign, hash, etc.)
